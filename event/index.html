Rules

FAQ

Frequently asked questions about Minecraft Tournament, its schedule, how to participate and so on and so on. Feel free to take a look! If you have any questions that weren't answered or you just want to chat feel free to join our Discord!

  • What is Minecraft Tournament?

    Minecraft Tournament is a privatly hosted competetive event for Minecraft: Java Edition

  • How does it work?

    7 teams with 5 players each compete against each other in minigames. The better you play, the more points you get. Each team has a team score, which is calculated from the points of the individual players. The 2 teams with the most points play against each other in the finale. The winner of the finale wins Minecraft Tournament!

  • How can i participate?

    The event is private, meaning we decide who we pick out of all application. You can apply to compete in the event on our Participation Form . We will contact you if you are accepted!
